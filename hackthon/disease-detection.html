Know the symptoms and solutions to common plant diseases

üçÇ Leaf Blight

Symptoms:

Yellow or brown spots on leaves, leading to wilting and drying.

Solution:

Remove infected leaves, avoid overhead watering, and use fungicides containing copper or chlorothalonil.

üåæ Powdery Mildew

Symptoms:

White, powdery fungal growth on leaves and stems, stunted growth.

Solution:

Increase air circulation, apply sulfur-based or neem oil spray early in the infection.

üåø Downy Mildew

Symptoms:

Pale green to yellow patches on upper leaf surfaces, grayish mold underneath.

Solution:

Improve drainage, reduce humidity, and apply copper-based fungicides.

ü¶† Bacterial Wilt

Symptoms:

Sudden wilting of leaves without yellowing, brown streaks in stems.

Solution:

Remove infected plants, avoid replanting in the same area, and solarize soil if possible.

ü™± Root Rot

Symptoms:

Wilting, yellowing leaves, mushy brown roots, foul odor.

Solution:

Improve drainage, let soil dry between waterings, and treat with a biological fungicide like Trichoderma.

üêõ Aphid Infestation

Symptoms:

Sticky leaves, distorted growth, presence of small insects on undersides of leaves.

Solution:

Spray plants with water or neem oil, introduce natural predators like ladybugs.
